Important Information

Eligibility

All Indian citizens aged 18 years and above are eligible to vote.

Voter ID Required

Your Voter ID card is required to cast your vote online.

One Vote Per Person

Each voter can cast only one vote. Duplicate voting is strictly prohibited.

Secure Process

All votes are encrypted and stored securely. Your vote is confidential.

How to Vote Online
